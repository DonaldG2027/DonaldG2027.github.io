What is Get To School?

Get to School was a simple game that had one simple task: getting to school.

What's the big deal?

The kid you play as, Reid, has some... issues. He's a heavy sleeper, and his parents are sick of giving him rides to school because of how much he misses the bus. So, they set up the perfect alarm that they designed to where the possibility that he sleeps through it is IMPOSSIBLE.

What happens?

In a very unexpected situation, Reid somehow sleeps through the alarm and once again misses the bus. His parents are so baffled by this that they are too frustrated to give him a ride. So now, Reid has to get to school on his own. However, since he doesn't have his license, it's not going to be an easy task.

So what now?

Well, that's where you come in. You have to help Reid get to school on time. This won't be as easy as it seems, since there are multiple paths to traverse and enemies to fight, and some surprising twists await you. BUT, if you can help him get there, it will be worth it.
